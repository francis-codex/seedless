# Tally form setup — #GoSeedless

When you log into tally.so, create a new form with these exact settings.

---

## Form title

**Submit your #GoSeedless review**

## Form description (shown above the fields)

```
You completed the 5 tasks and posted on X with #GoSeedless. Drop your post link + the wallet address where you want USDC sent if you win.

25 random winners. Top 3 reports get $15 instead of $5. Draw Sat Jun 27 night, payouts Sun Jun 28.
```

---

## Field 1 — X post link

- **Type:** URL
- **Label:** Your X post link
- **Help text:** Paste the full link to your X post. It must tag @francis_codex, tag @seedless_wallet, and use #GoSeedless. Posts without those will be skipped.
- **Required:** YES

## Field 2 — USDC wallet address

- **Type:** Short text
- **Label:** USDC wallet address (Solana)
- **Help text:** Solana address where we'll send USDC if you win. Make sure this is a wallet YOU control. Triple-check it — payouts go to the address as you typed it.
- **Required:** YES

---

## Submit button label

**Enter the draw**

---

## Auto-response (shown after submission)

```
You're in.

Random draw happens Saturday Jun 27 night. The 3 best reports out of the 25 winners get $15 instead of $5. Winners announced Sunday Jun 28 on X — keep an eye on @seedless_wallet and @francis_codex.

Thanks for testing Seedless.

— the team
```

---

## Settings to flip ON

- Close form: **Saturday Jun 27, 12:00 WAT**
- Send confirmation email: optional (only if you ask for an email field — we're not)
- Limit one response per email: NOT applicable since we don't collect email
- Open form to anyone: YES (no Tally account required to submit)

Copy the public form URL — that goes into the recruitment tweet and the TG pin.
