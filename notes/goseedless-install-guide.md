# Installing Seedless v0.4.5-beta on Android

You're getting an APK file because Seedless is still in private mainnet beta. Here's how to install it in 5 steps.

---

## Step 1 — Download the APK

Tap the link in the beta tester Telegram group. Your phone downloads the `.apk` file (around 80 MB).

## Step 2 — Allow installs from your browser

When you tap the file, Android may say:

> "For your security, your phone isn't allowed to install unknown apps from this source."

Tap **Settings**, toggle **Allow from this source** ON, then tap back.

## Step 3 — Install

Tap **Install**. Wait a few seconds. Tap **Open** when it finishes.

## Step 4 — Create your wallet

Seedless will ask you to create a passkey.

This IS your wallet. No 12 words to write down, no seed phrase to lose. Just your fingerprint or Face ID.

Follow the prompts.

## Step 5 — You're in

The app opens to your new wallet.

- Tap **Receive** to copy your wallet address or show your QR
- Tap **Send** to send to anyone
- Tap **History** to see your activity
- Use the **Swap** icon in the bottom nav to swap tokens
- Switch to the **Tools** tab (next to Tokens) for **Stealth address** and **Burner wallet**

---

## Troubleshooting

**"Can't install"**
Make sure you have ~150 MB free storage and Android 8.0 or newer.

**Passkey prompt doesn't work**
Make sure your phone has biometric unlock set up (fingerprint or Face ID). Add one in Settings → Security if you haven't.

**App crashes on first launch**
DM us in the beta tester group, we'll help.

**"Network error" on first balance check**
First request can take a few seconds to wake up. Pull down to refresh.
