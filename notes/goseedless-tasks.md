# #GoSeedless — Your 5 tester tasks

Do all 5 in any order. Should take about 10-15 minutes.

---

## 1. Open the app and make your wallet

Passkey-based, no seed phrase. Just your fingerprint or Face ID.

## 2. Get some crypto into it

Send any token to your new wallet from another wallet, or have a friend send something. Any amount works.

## 3. Send some to anyone

To a friend, your other wallet, or any address you have. Tiny amount is fine — point is to feel the flow.

## 4. Do a swap

Tap the **Swap** icon in the bottom nav. Pick any pair (SOL ↔ USDC, SOL → SEED, whatever you want).

## 5. Try a stealth address OR a burner wallet

In the **Tools** tab, pick one:
- **Stealth address** — generate a one-time address, share it, see incoming
- **Burner wallet** — make a throwaway wallet, send through it
