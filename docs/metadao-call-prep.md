## Your Pitch (60 Seconds)

Seedless is a passkey-native Solana wallet that replaces seed phrases with biometrics. No keys to write down, no gas fees, no complicated setup. Just your fingerprint or Face ID.

We're building for the next 100 million wallet users in emerging markets — people who have smartphones but no bank account and no patience for seed phrases. Starting with Nigeria and East Africa.

We've shipped 3 beta phases with real users testing. Mainnet is pending a final infrastructure audit from our partner (LazorKit, coordinating with Solana Foundation). The product is built and ready.

We already have a community of ~300 people, an existing token ($SEED on Bags), and deep roots in Nigeria's Superteam Solana community.

We're looking at MetaDAO because the market-governed treasury and performance-based unlocks align perfectly with what we stand for — trust, security, and accountability.

## Why MetaDAO Fits Seedless

1. **Brand alignment** — a wallet built on trust launching on a platform built on anti-rug protections. The narrative writes itself.
2. **Performance-based unlocks** — we don't get paid unless the token goes up. That's conviction, not extraction.
3. **Bid wall** — gives early investors a price floor. Lowers the risk of backing an early-stage project.
4. **Futarchy governance** — more sophisticated than token voting. Signals that Seedless is serious about doing things right.
5. **Legal entity** — real investor protections, not just vibes.

## Target User

People in Africa who send and receive money daily but find current crypto wallets too complicated to start.

Specifically:
- Someone who uses M-Pesa or Opay daily but has never held crypto because the setup is intimidating
- A freelancer or small business owner who wants to receive USDC payments without dealing with seed phrases and gas fees
- Someone who's heard of crypto but got scared off by the 12-word setup or lost money because of it

The next 100 million wallet users aren't in San Francisco. They're in Lagos, Nairobi, and Accra. Seedless is built for them.

## Quick Reference — MetaDAO Mechanics

- **ICO**: 4 days, 10M tokens, proportional distribution, refund if minimum not hit
- **Treasury**: all USDC goes to market-governed treasury, not founder
- **Team tokens**: up to 12.9M (50% supply), unlock at 2x/4x/8x/16x/32x ICO price, 18-month cliff
- **Bid wall**: excess raise funds a price floor, tokens burned on sell-back, 90 days
- **Budget**: monthly team budget capped at 1/6th of minimum raise
- **IP**: domains, software, socials transferred to project entity
- **Governance**: futarchy — traders bet on outcomes, 3-day trading period, TWAP decides
