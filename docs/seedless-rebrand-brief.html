Seedless Rebrand Brief

Brand Identity Refresh — Phase 4
From: Francis (Seedless Labs)
To: Wells (Designer)
Date: April 14, 2026
Direction

Shifting Seedless from "developer / crypto tool" to "consumer app anyone could use." Think warm, friendly, approachable — like a fintech app that happens to be crypto. Someone who has never touched crypto should see the brand and think "this looks friendly, I'd try this" — not "this is for coders."

Core Message

No seed phrase. Just your face / fingers.

Specifications

Logo Direction

Open to wordmark, icon, or combo — leaning toward clean lowercase wordmark + small icon mark that works solo as app icon and twitter pfp.

Mode

Light mode first (matches warm/friendly direction). Dark mode as secondary — both should feel intentional, not just inverted colors.

Illustrations

Flat or soft 3D — subtle, premium, minimal. Should support UI, not dominate it. No heavy crypto iconography (no chains, locks, blockchain cubes).

Revisions

2 rounds included. Round 1: explore 2-3 directions. Round 2: refine the chosen direction.

Keep

  • ✓ The name "Seedless"
  • ✓ Core message: no seed phrase, just your face/fingers
  • ✓ Friendly, human, approachable tone

Kill

  • ✕ Heavy dark/purple "crypto hacker" aesthetic
  • ✕ Anything that looks like a developer tool
  • ✕ "Infrastructure" language in any visual
  • ✕ Cold, technical, unapproachable energy
"Someone who has never touched crypto should see this brand and want to try it."
