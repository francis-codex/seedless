Seedless — Pitch Video Script

~2:30 · ~300 words at 115 wpm · slide-aligned · pause ½s on each slide change

Slide 1 — Title 0:00 – 0:10

Hey, I'm Francis. With my co-founder Udoka, we're building the wallet your mom could actually use on Solana. It's called Seedless. The wallet with no seed phrase.

Slide 2 — Problem 0:10 – 0:25

Every wallet today asks you to write down 12 random words on paper. Lose them, your money is gone. Sixty-one percent of new users give up at that screen and run back to a custodial wallet.

Slide 3 — Solution 0:25 – 0:40

We replaced the seed phrase with your face. One scan, you have a real Solana wallet on mainnet. Send, receive, swap, even send privately, all from one app on your phone.

Slide 4 — Retention moat 0:40 – 0:55

But here's the part that keeps people. From the same wallet, you'll send straight to any bank account. Crypto in, cash out. No off-ramp queue, no app switching. NGN first, in private beta.

Slide 5 — Three layers 0:55 – 1:05

The stack underneath is real. Native passkeys on iOS and Android. LazorKit for smart-wallet rails on Solana. Umbra for privacy, live on mainnet.

Slide 6 — What no one else does 1:05 – 1:25

Four things every other wallet makes hard, we made simple. Sign with your face, not a phrase. Spend without thinking about gas. Send privately by default. Recover without a backup file. We're not trying to be a better Phantom. We're your bank, on crypto rails.

Demo cut-in 1:25 – 1:45

Twenty seconds of the live app: FaceID, gas-free swap, burner wallet, private send, stealth payment QR. All on mainnet, no seed phrase touched.

Slide 7 — Who we're for 1:45 – 1:55

We're built for the hundred million who installed a wallet and never used it, and the next billion who need money, not investments.

Slide 8 — Revenue 1:55 – 2:05

We make money two ways. Zero point seven five percent on every swap, below the leader. Plus one percent on every fiat-to-crypto on-ramp.

Slide 10 — Real numbers 2:05 – 2:15

Today: fifty to a hundred beta users on private mainnet. Over a thousand passkey signatures on chain. Two hundred USDG from Superteam. Twenty-five thousand in Alchemy credits.

Slide 11 — Why us 2:15 – 2:25

Four months, idea to Solana mainnet. I built payments at Paystack, a perp DEX at sub-100ms, a Wormhole bridge doing two hundred K a month. Udoka ran SuperteamNG community to thirty K GDP. We've shipped every layer Seedless sits on.

Slide 13 — Close 2:25 – 2:30

Seedless. The wallet your mom could actually use. Join the beta at seedlesslabs.xyz.
